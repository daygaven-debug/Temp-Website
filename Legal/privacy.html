Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025

Ad-It (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website, user portal, and related services (the “Platform”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, login credentials, and linked social media accounts.
  • User Content: Media, text, and other content you upload to the Platform.
  • Usage Data: Log data, device information, IP addresses, cookies, and analytics data about your interactions with the Platform.
  • Payment Information: Transaction history, payment method details, and payout records (processed via third-party providers).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Platform.

3. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share anonymized or aggregated analytics with advertisers and partners.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (encryption, access controls, monitoring) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

  • Access and update your account information at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Opt out of non-essential cookies or marketing communications.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Platform is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. International Users

If you access the Platform from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the U.S., which may have different data protection laws.
